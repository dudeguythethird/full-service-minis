Welcome to Full Service Minis! We offer fully painted Warhammer for your gaming needs. We stock a selection of popular kits that you can get painted to a variety of quality grades. We also offer custom paint jobs. These can be for any miniatures you desire and can also be as detailed as you see fit!

Our fully painted miniatures come in three distinct tiers of paint work:

Game Ready

  • Our cheapest painting and construction tier. This is ideal if you want a nice looking set of miniatures without breaking the bank.
  • A great option for those that just want to get their new minis on the tabletop.
  • Includes basic texture work for your mini's bases, if applicable.
  • Includes a base coat of up to 6 colours, recess shading and drybrushing.

Game Ready Enhanced

  • A step above Game Ready in both price and quality.
  • This tier is designed for those that want painted miniatures for tabletop play, but want them to be a bit more striking than the norm.
  • Includes texture work on your mini's bases, as well as battlefield debris, if applicable.
  • Includes a base coat of up to 9 colours, recess shading, drybrushing, and brush highlighting for extra impact.

Custom

  • This tier is only available through a unique order form.
  • This form can also be used to order kits we don't stock (painted to whatever grade you desire).
  • The sky is the limit in terms of detail and non-standard features. Want dead ork on your marine's base? You need only ask.
  • If wanted, we will use advance techniques, like wet blending, in custom paint jobs.
  • The prices for these kinds of jobs can vary wildly, and will be decided in consultation between us and the client. When a price is reached, it can be payed here.
