Painting Tier Pricing Guide

In all kinds of job, custom and standard, the price of the job is determined by the price of the kit that is being worked on. On top of this, there are charges for assembly, painting, and any custom features you want to add. Standard assembly charges are a flat +50% on top of the cost of the kit/s that you want made. Painting, on the other hand, comes in a variety of quality bands, detailed below:

Game Ready (+50% to base price)

This tier consists of a zenithal base coat, up to 6 colours on the miniature, recess shading, and basic highlights achieved with dry brushing. At this tier, bases will be coated with one of a few, simple, natural terrain types.

Game Ready Enhanced (+100% to base price)

This tier consists of a zenithal base coat, up to 9 colours on the miniature, recess shading, basic highlights achieved with dry brushing, and complex highlighting applied with careful thought. At this tier, bases will be coated with one of a few simple, natural terrain types, along with your choice of battlefield debris, strewn haphazardly. You can also choose a rolled pattern.

Custom (Costs vary)

This is a painting tier that is only available in custom jobs. The sky is limit in terms of quality, you can expect all of the above and more.
